### 🚀 About me

I run **Draw2Code**, a small web and AI agency with a base in Chandigarh and a Canadian presence. I work hands-on as the technical builder — architecture, backend, integrations — while also leading client conversations end-to-end.

My current focus is shipping production systems that combine:
- **Backend engineering** — Java, Spring Boot, Node.js, REST APIs, payment flows
- **AI automation** — voice agents, outbound calling infrastructure, structured data extraction
- **Web3 / DeFi tooling** — Solidity, smart contracts, multi-chain integrations
- **Custom websites** — performant, conversion-focused builds for service businesses
